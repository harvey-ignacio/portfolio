About Me

Developer, designer,
and problem finder.

I'm Harvey — a web developer and designer with 6+ years of experience helping businesses build and improve their online presence. I started in WordPress and ecommerce, but my work spans full website builds, Shopify stores, UI design, and graphics.

I don't just write code. I look at how a site is structured, how users move through it, and whether the design actually communicates what the business needs. Then I fix what's broken and build what's missing.
